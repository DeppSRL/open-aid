The process of defining post 2015 development goals (UN post 2015 Agenda) should be completed during a summit to be convened in September 2015. This process also includes the one launched at the Rio +20 Conference for sustainable development goals. A key aspect of this process will consist of defining funding for the new development agenda.

In this context, Italy is proactive in the ongoing debate both internationally and nationally. At the latter level, it has begun a consultation process within the “Inter-Institutional Committee on Cooperation for Development” with the participation of all actors of the Italian Cooperation. Both the Italian Presidency of the EU in the second half of 2014 and Expo Milano 2015 will be held at a crucial stage of the negotiation process for the new agenda.
